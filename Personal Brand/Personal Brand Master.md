## What Works

- **Sends** are the #1 algorithmic signal — create content people forward
- **Watch time** is the primary distribution lever — hook must work in 3 seconds
- **22 Habits of Men Who Become Undeniable** Reel = strongest performer to date
- Average watch time currently ~15 seconds on 74-second video — this is the main thing to fix

---

## Content Formula

- Talking-head / selfie style
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Value delivery (systems, habits, discipline, faith, style)
- Soft CTA at end
- Cross-post to Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts simultaneously

---

## Technical Rules

- Export at 1080p/30fps highest bitrate (not 4K — no benefit, bigger file)
- Never text or DM video files (recompresses each pass)
- Use AirDrop or Google Drive/Dropbox to transfer files
- Post directly from your own device

---

## Conversion Gap Problem

Strong engagement. Low follower growth.  
**Root cause:** People watch and send but don't follow.  
**Fix:** End of video must give a clear reason to follow ("follow for more of this")
