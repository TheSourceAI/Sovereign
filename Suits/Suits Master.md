## The Business

---

## Sales Approach

- Warm, low-pressure messaging
- Always anchor outreach to a concrete hook (new fabric arrival, referral, specific occasion)
- Move prospects toward a phone call BEFORE sending anything formal
- Never mention contracts until after a real conversation

---

## Outreach Formula

1. Reference something specific about them
2. Mention the hook (new fabric, referral, occasion)
3. Soft CTA — 2-3 sentences max
4. Move to phone call
5. Schedule fitting
